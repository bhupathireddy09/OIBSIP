# Social Engineering Attacks

## Objective
To study common social engineering techniques and understand their impact on cybersecurity.

## Topics Covered
- Phishing
- Spear Phishing
- Vishing
- Smishing
- Baiting
- Pretexting

## Prevention Measures
- Security Awareness Training
- Multi-Factor Authentication
- Identity Verification
- Safe Browsing Practices

## Conclusion
Understanding social engineering attacks helps individuals and organizations defend against human-focused cyber threats.
