# Patch Management

## Objective

To understand the importance of patch management and its role in maintaining cybersecurity.

## Topics Covered

- Patch Management Process
- Security Updates
- Vulnerability Management
- Risks of Delayed Patching
- Best Practices

## Benefits

- Improved Security
- Reduced Vulnerabilities
- Better System Stability
- Regulatory Compliance

## Conclusion

Effective patch management helps organizations protect their systems and maintain a secure IT environment.
